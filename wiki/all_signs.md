# United Nations General Assembly
## Resolution 884: Regulation of Outer-Planet Mining Concessions
### Solar Confederation Official Decree

**Date:** 14 October 2248
**Origin:** UN General Assembly, Lunar Seat

**Article 1: Scope and Jurisdiction**
This decree outlines the administrative and operational requirements for all commercial mining operations within the designated Kuiper Belt Resource Zones.

**Article 2: Environmental Compliance**
All participating entities must submit comprehensive environmental impact assessments to the Solar Confederation Ecological Oversight Committee prior to the deployment of extraction vessels.

**Article 3: Labor Standards**
The Confederation guarantees standard life-support subsidies and hazard pay multipliers for all personnel deployed to Class-C orbital habitats or lower.

**Article 4: Enforcement**
Failure to comply with these regulations will result in the immediate revocation of transit vectors and the freezing of all associated central banking assets.

# Sovereign Trade Agreement
## The Federated States of Kisangani & The Jovian Merchant Guild
### Legal Charter of Inter-System Commerce

**Ratification Date:** 03 Cycle 4, Post-Independence Era
**Location:** Capital City of New Kinshasa, Planet Kisangani

**Section I: Declaration of Sovereign Exchange**
This document establishes the formal *ruhanda* (trajectories) for mutual exchange of agricultural biotech and heavy orbital machinery between the Federated States of Kisangani and the Jovian Merchant Guild.

**Section II: Automated Systems Protocol**
Both parties agree that all automated cargo drones entering Kisangani sovereign space must be placed into a state of *uzororo* (automated rest/standby) upon crossing the outer defense perimeter, allowing for manual inspection by Kisangani Port Authorities.

**Section III: Tariff Structures**
A standard ten percent planetary development tariff will be applied to all imported Jovian machinery, to fund the ongoing terraforming initiatives of the Federated States.

**Section IV: Dispute Resolution**
Any conflicts arising from this agreement will be mediated by a neutral council appointed by the African Continental Union, honoring the decolonial statecraft principles upon which Kisangani was founded.
