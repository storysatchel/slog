# United Nations General Assembly
## Resolution 884: Regulation of Outer-Planet Mining Concessions
### Solar Confederation Official Decree

**Date:** 14 October 2448
**Origin:** UN General Assembly, Lunar Seat

**Article 1: Scope and Jurisdiction**
This decree outlines the administrative and operational requirements for all commercial mining operations within the designated Kuiper Belt Resource Zones.

**Article 2: Environmental Compliance**
All participating entities must submit comprehensive environmental impact assessments to the Solar Confederation Ecological Oversight Committee prior to the deployment of extraction vessels.

**Article 3: Labor Standards**
The Confederation guarantees standard life-support subsidies and hazard pay multipliers for all personnel deployed to Class-C orbital habitats or lower.

**Article 4: Enforcement**
Failure to comply with these regulations will result in the immediate revocation of transit vectors and the freezing of all associated central banking assets.
