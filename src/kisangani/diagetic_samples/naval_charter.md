# The Edict of the Hondo (FSK Naval Command)

The defense of the Federated States of Kisangani operates as a *Hondo* (Host/War), strictly rejecting the colonial structures of Admirals and Captains favored by the Solar Confederation. The hierarchy reflects the historical military organization of the Mutapa and Rozvi states, ensuring absolute structural sovereignty.

**The Roster of the Host:**
1. **Nduna Huru (Great Commanders):** The fleet commanders who hold the astropolitical borders.
2. **Nduna (Commanders):** The ship captains who command individual vessels.
3. **Mukuru (Seniors/Officers):** The operational officers guiding the *Njeredura* (Expert Systems) and *Uropi* (Gel Software).
4. **Mutoa (Warriors/Marines):** The shock troops trained for high-G combat and zero-G boarding, sustained by the *N'anga's muto*.
