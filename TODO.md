# Conlang Master Plan: Humanity's Slice of the Galaxy (2750)

This document tracks our progress in fleshing out the diverse sociolinguistic landscape across the human diaspora using the Audition tool.

## 1. Regional "Basics"
- [ ] **Kisangani Basic** (Federated States of Kisangani)
  - *Context:* Founded by African colonists; heavy focus on archaeological/historiographic roots.
  - *Features:* Lexicon heavily influenced by diverse African languages.
- [ ] **Eridian Basic** (Commonwealth of Lotannia & Castoran National State)
  - *Context:* Spoken in the Ran system. (Distinct from the alien species).
- [ ] **Aurelian Basic** (Aurelian Confederacy)
  - *Context:* Decentralized language spoken alongside English.

## 2. Fast-Evolving & Isolationist Languages
- [ ] **Sperosi** (Sperosi Federation on o2 Eridani)
  - *Context:* Highly isolationist jungle planet.
  - *Features:* Evolved faster than most of Earth's former colonies; distinct morphological shifts.

## 3. Creole and Pidgin Descendants
- [ ] **Kubileyan** (Free Territory of Kubileya)
  - *Context:* Descendant of Belter Creole (*lang Belta*).
  - *Features:* Pidgin combining Russian, Esperanto, Korean, Chinese, Portuguese, Spanish, French, Dutch, German, Italian, Hungarian, Turkish, Arabic. Heavily incorporates physical vac-suit gestures.
