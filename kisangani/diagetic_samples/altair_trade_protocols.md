> **Article IV: __mbengo hewa kuruva__**
> 
> **1. __ruhanda__:**
> __sheria taifa kisangani, vyombo solar kuhaingia hewa. biashara soko juu sika.__
> 
> **2. __kosa__:**
> __kosa ruhanda mutyairi kuleta futo juu. vyombo uzororo mamlaka KFN.__
> 
> **3. __kuruva mbizi__:**
> __wazungu kuhaingia bahari altair. biashara, wazungu kuleta kuruva waupanda.__
